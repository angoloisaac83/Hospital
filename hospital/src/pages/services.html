The Christ Hospital Health Network Volunteer Services Department

​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​The Christ Hospital’s mission is to improve the health of our community and create value by providing exceptional outcomes, affordable care, and the finest experiences. Our attention to patient experience that’s founded in clinical excellence is why U.S. News and World Report ranks us one of America’s Best year after year. And why The Christ Hospital has been named the Most Preferred Hospital by the Greater Cincinnati community for 21 consecutive years (National Research Corporation).

Volunteers have been a vital part of The Christ Hospital family since 1894. Our mission is to “Increase and sustain the volunteer population at The Christ Hospital Health Network through identifying external and internal opportunities while developing strong relationships within the community so that we become the volunteer organization of choice. Create a mutually beneficial partnership between the volunteer and The Christ Hospital in order to provide the finest experiences”.

Our commitment to finest patient experience is at the heart of our mission and vision. The volunteers’ knowledge of The Christ Hospital needs, services, and goals makes them valuable public relations representatives.​

Meet some of our volunteers and see how they are making a difference here at The Christ Hospital.​​​

      • There is no typical volunteer at The Christ Hospital. Each person is different, bringing special skills and talents to the job. Our volunteers are men, women, retirees, employed persons, college students and high school students. Our volunteers give thousands of service hours to The Christ Hospital Health Network.

      Company Involvement

      The Christ Hospital Volunteer Department is actively seeking employees from various businesses and corporations in the Greater Cincinnati area. Possible opportunities include Patient Gift Drives (check out what we need here​​​), Adopting a Garden at our main campus in Mt. Auburn (which would entail light gardening work), Weekend Encouragers (patient interaction), just to name a few. If you or your company is interested, please contact volunteer@thechristhospital.com​ or 513-585-2244.

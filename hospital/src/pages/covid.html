COVID-19
COVID-19 UPDATES

​Mask and Face Covering Guidelines

Masking is currently optional in all public and clinical spaces. The following exceptions may apply:

  • If you are experiencing symptoms of a respiratory illness like influenza or COVID-19, you may be asked to wear a mask to prevent the spread of illness to others.
  • Physicians may order masking for specific inpatient rooms.
  • Any patient may request that anyone entering their room to wear a mask.
  • Patients in our Cancer Center or oncology offices may request social distancing from those not wearing a mask.
  • Masking in clinical areas will be mandatory during flu season.

Visitor's Restriction

  • There are currently no visitor restrictions at the hospital or our physician practices.

    • Testing

  • Contact your primary care provider if you have COVID-19 symptoms and are interested in a COVID-19 test for confirmation. Testing is also available at The Christ Hospital Urgent Care Centers.

    Please note the cost of COVID-19 testing will be billed to you or your insurance just like other testing. You are responsible for any applicable co-pay, co-insurance or other costs not covered by your insurance plan.

    • Vaccines & Boosters

  • If you have not been vaccinated for COVID-19 or are due for a booster, you can schedule an appointment through your patient portal under “Schedule an Appointment." You can also call your primary care office directly to ask about scheduling. ​The Christ Hospital Apothecary, our full-service retail pharmacy located on our main campus in Mt. Auburn, also offers vaccines and boosters by appointment.
    • Schedule through the Ohio Department of Health website.
    • Search by Zip code 45219.
    • Select The Christ Hospital Apothecary to find available appointments.
    Call our Patient & Guest Services for any questions or concerns
